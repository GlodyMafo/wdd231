Website Plan – 243 Direct News

Site Name

243 Direct News

This name was chosen because it directly reflects the goal of the website: to deliver up-to-date news and information about the Democratic Republic of Congo in a clear and accessible way.

Optional domain availability: 243directnews.cd

Site Purpose

The purpose of Congo Actualités is to provide Congolese citizens and the diaspora with simplified access to national news and current events. The website offers a structured view of local politics, culture, economy, sports, and more — all through a modern, mobile-friendly, and accessible interface using only front-end technologies.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the latest political news from Congo?
  • How can I mark certain articles as favorites to read later?

Color Schema

The site will use the following color palette:

  • Primary Color – Navy Blue (#1e3d59): Used for headings, navigation bar, and footer background.
  • Accent Color – Warm Yellow (#ffb400): Used for buttons, highlights, and underlines.
  • important Color – Red (#ad0d0d): Used for important lines.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif): Used for main headings and section titles.
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text and navigation links.
